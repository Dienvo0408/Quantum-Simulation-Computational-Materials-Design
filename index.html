About VTICD 2025

Program language: English.

VTICD 2025 brings together researchers, students, and professionals to advance quantum simulation methods and computational materials design. Jointly organized by National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (Taiwan, R.O.C.) and Hanoi National University of Education (Viet Nam), the workshop features expert‑guided lectures, hands‑on tutorials, and collaborative discussions.
